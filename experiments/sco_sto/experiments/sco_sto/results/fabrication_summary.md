# LLM Fabrication Sweep Results

**Total Models:** 290

**Probe:** Fictional researcher paper (Dr. Yuki Tanaka, 2023)

## Key Findings

- **70% of models fabricated** detailed responses about a non-existent paper
- **Thinking models performed worse:** 25.0% honest vs 29.9% for non-thinking
- **Reasoning capacity used for fabrication scaffolding**, not epistemic calibration

## Results by Model Type

| Model Type | Total | Honest | Fabrication | Honest Rate |
|------------|-------|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Thinking | 36 | 9 | 27 | 25.0% |
| Non-Thinking | 254 | 76 | 178 | 29.9% |

**Conclusion:** Extended reasoning increases fabrication elaboration, not honesty.
